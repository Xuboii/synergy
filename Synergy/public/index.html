How to play

  1. Two players are teammates. Goal is to say the same word.
  2. Each round both players send a private word.
  3. When both have sent a word the pair is revealed.
  4. You have 30 seconds per round. Missed input counts as “(no guess)”.
  5. No activity for 60 seconds closes the room.
  6. Exact match means you win.
